Goodbye & Good Riddance - Juice WRLD

Rating: 7/10

Album Review

Juice WRLD's album is a solid 7/10, offering a mix of emotional depth and catchy beats. The late rapper's introspective tackles issues of heartbreak, struggle and the dark side of fame, the vocals bring a raw authenticity to the songs and Juice WRLD’s voice stand out creates memorable moments, creating an emotional connection with the listener. But the album sometimes relies heavily on specific sounds, leaving some songs feeling a bit stylized. While there are outstanding moments that showcase Juice WRLD’s genius, there is still room for versatility across the sonic palette. Despite these nuances, the album stands as a testament to Juice WRLD’s impact on the genre and its ability to connect with fans through authenticity and relatability

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy - Kanye West

Rating: 9/10

Album Review

Kanye West's "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy" is nothing short of a masterpiece, as it received a well-deserved 9/10. This album is a sonic journey that pushes the boundaries of hip-hop, blending genres with ease. Kanye's production is incredible to say the least. Guest features, from Jay-Z to Bon Iver, hit hard and are extremely well done. Each track feels like a carefully crafted work of art, contributing to a narrative of self-reflection and social critique. The album’s bold and experimental quality sets it apart. It can at times feel chaotic and reach for too much but so is Kanye. "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy" is a testament to just how great Kanye West is.

Stankonia - Outkast

Rating: 10/10

Album review

"Stankonia" by OutKast is one of the greatest rap albums of all time for a reaosn. There is no doubt deserving a perfect 10/10. The album is an innovation of Hip Hop. OutKast fearlessly blends genres, infusing funk, soul, and electronic elements into their signature Southern sound. Tracks like "B.O.B." and "Ms. Jackson" hit hard with nice beats and rhymes, showcasing the duo's lyrical ability. The production is next-level, with each song bringing a fresh and unpredictable vibe. "Stankonia" is not just an album; it's a cultural phenomenon that defies conventions and takes listeners on a wild ride.

The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill - Lauryn Hill

Rating: 8/10

Album Review

Lauryn Hill's "The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill" is a solid 8/10, standing as a landmark in the world of hip-hop and R&B. The combo of R&B, soul, and hip-hop elements creates a really well done album. Standout tracks like "Doo Wop” and "Ex-Factor" showcase Hill's ability to blend storytelling with melodies. However, the album's repetitive themes can in some cases get annoying. Despite this, "The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill" remains a timeless classic, earning its place as a pivotal work that has influenced countless artists in the years since its release.
