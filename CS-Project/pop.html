Abbey Road

Artist: The Beatles

Rating: 10/10

Album Review

To say that Abbey Road is a good influential album would be an offensive understatement. With this record, the Beatles unleashed the formula that would define modern music forever. Some may even argue that this album marked the start of a new era in music. With its catchy and appealing choruses to beautiful instrumentals, there is no doubt in my mind that this album has changed the course of music forever. While many older albums crafted around the same time have become outdated and have begun to sound monotone, The Beatles built an LP that was meant to last forever and has engraved itself into countless listeners.
